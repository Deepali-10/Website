What to expect in an Ayurvedic consultation

 

A consultation involves an in-depth health assessment using traditional Ayurvedic diagnostic methods. In an Ayurvedic consultation we practice Trividha Pariksha, which are the three ways of gaining knowledge. They are Darshana (observation), Prashna (questioning) and Sparshana (touching).

What is the outcome of an Ayurvedic consultation?

 

We will explain your health issues to you from an Ayurvedic perspective, in a way that you can understand. We will develop a suitable course of treatment to suit you. This could be dietary and lifestyle changes, Ayurvedic herbal medicine, Ayurvedic massage or therapies, Kalari Chikitsa or a combination of these.

What should I bring to an Ayurvedic consultation?

 

If you have scans and medical reports related to your condition, please bring them. Please bring a list of any medications and supplements you are taking.
