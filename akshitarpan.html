AKSHITARPAN THERAPY

 

Askhi means eyes and tarpan means nourishment, giving strength to the eyes.

Akshi Tarpana, also known as Netra Vasti, is a process of keeping medicated ghee or oil on the eyes for a certain period of time.It is the best purification and rejuvenation process of eye and other sense organs. In healthy individuals it is carried out to protect the eyes from degeneration due to the aging process and to improve the vision in the diseased person. Akshi tarpan is one of the specialized treatments for various eye disorders. Akshi tarpan acts as a preventative and curative medical aid for maintaining perfect health. Akshi tarpan is a procedure where a warm medicated ghee (clarified butter) is made to stay stagnant within the eyes for a speculated time in a specific formed frame.

 

Benefits of the Treatment

 

A few benefits of Panchakarma Treatment are:

  • It is said to be an excellent treatment to improve blurred vision.
  • Removes dark circles beneath the eyes.
  • Revitalises and rejuvenates tired, dry, rough and injured eyes.
  • Reduces burning sensation.
  • Strengthens the nerves and muscles of the eyes.

Recommended for:

  • Burning in the eyes
  • Dry eye syndrome
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Pain in the eyes
  • Watering and cloudiness of the eyes
  • Refractive errors of the eyes.
